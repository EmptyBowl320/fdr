The Death of Franklin Delano Roosevelt

Franklin D. Rooselvelt died in April 12, 1945 in Warm Springs, GA. The cause of his death is Cerebral hemorrhage(Cerebral bleed), a type of intracranical bleed that occurs witin the brain tissue or ventricle. Rooselvelt's sudden death shook the American public; though many had noticed that he looked exhausted in photographs and newsreels, no one seemed prepared for his passing. By dying at the climax of war, Rooselvelt, like Abraham Lincoln passed from the scene at the moment of the greatest personal glory.
